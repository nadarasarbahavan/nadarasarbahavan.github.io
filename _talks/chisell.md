**Instructor for 5-week introductory workshop on CHISEL and Scala for 30+ undergraduate students**
<!-- <img src="https://github.com/nadarasarbahavan/nadarasarbahavan.github.io/blob/master/images/talk.png" width="400"> -->
1. Designed and delivered a 5-week introductory workshop on CHISEL and Scala for 30+ undergraduate students.
2. Introduced Scala fundamentals relevant to hardware design, including classes, objects, collections, and parameterized hardware construction.
3. Taught core CHISEL concepts and demonstrated the differences between traditional HDLs (Verilog/SystemVerilog) and generator-based hardware design.
4. Guided students in setting up the CHISEL development environment and implementing basic combinational and sequential digital circuits.
