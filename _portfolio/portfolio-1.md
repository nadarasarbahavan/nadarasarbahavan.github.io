Surface electromyography (sEMG) is technique through which a bio-electrical signal generated from muscular contractions and relaxations is acquired from the surface of the skin. In this study, an sEMG acquisition device (with active shielding) was designed as a neural interface using dry stainless steel electrodes and an Adafruit Feather M0 WiFi board (Adafruit Industries,USA).

Presented at IEEE EMBC ISC 2021, as a Presentation and Extended Abstract

Collaborative work between Nadarasar Bahavan, Gevindu Wickramaarachchi, Kithmin Wickremasinghe
